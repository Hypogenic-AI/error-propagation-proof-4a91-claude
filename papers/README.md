# Downloaded Papers

## Correctly Downloaded and Verified

1. **The Invariant Extended Kalman Filter as a Stable Observer** (barrau_bonnabel_2014_IEKF.pdf)
   - Authors: Barrau, Bonnabel
   - Year: 2014
   - arXiv: 1410.1465
   - Why relevant: Foundational paper establishing log-linear error propagation on Lie groups. Theorem 2 (log-linear property) is the key result enabling spectral analysis of nonlinear error dynamics.

2. **A Class of Quantum Error-Correcting Codes Saturating the Quantum Hamming Bound** (gottesman_1996_stabilizer.pdf)
   - Authors: Gottesman
   - Year: 1996
   - arXiv: quant-ph/9604038
   - Why relevant: Establishes stabilizer formalism. Proves stabilizer must be abelian — key evidence that abelian structure enables error correction.

3. **Non-stabilizer Quantum Codes from Abelian Subgroups** (arvind_2002_abelian_subgroups.pdf)
   - Authors: Arvind, Kurur, Parthasarathy
   - Year: 2002
   - arXiv: quant-ph/0210097
   - Why relevant: Constructs error-correcting codes from abelian subgroups of non-abelian error groups.

4. **A Mathematical Framework for IMU Error Propagation** (barrau_bonnabel_2020_framework.pdf)
   - Authors: Barrau, Bonnabel
   - Year: 2020
   - arXiv: 1911.09134
   - Why relevant: Extends log-linearity framework to SE_2(3) Lie group. Demonstrates exact preintegration formulas leveraging group structure.

5. **Linear Observed Systems on Groups** (barrau_2019_linear_systems_groups.pdf)
   - Authors: Barrau, Bonnabel
   - Year: 2019
   - arXiv: 1904.09261
   - Why relevant: Defines "linear systems on groups" — generalizes linear systems theory to Lie groups.

6. **Uncertainty Propagation on Unimodular Matrix Lie Groups** (ye_chirikjian_2025_uncertainty_unimodular.pdf)
   - Authors: Ye, Jayaraman, Chirikjian
   - Year: 2025
   - Why relevant: General uncertainty propagation on unimodular Lie groups with exact mean/covariance formulas.

7. **Transversal Gates and Error Propagation in 3D Topological Codes** (bombin_2018_transversal.pdf)
   - Authors: Bombin
   - Year: 2018
   - arXiv: 1810.09575
   - Why relevant: Studies how errors propagate through sequential gate operations with algebraic structure.

8. **Random Walks on Groups** (diaconis_2003_random_walks_groups.pdf)
   - Authors: Diaconis
   - Year: 2003
   - Why relevant: Spectral gap theory for random walks on groups; connects group structure to convergence rates.

9. **Group-theoretic Error Mitigation** (zhao_miyake_2023_group_error_mitigation.pdf)
   - Authors: Zhao, Miyake
   - Year: 2023
   - arXiv: 2301.07233
   - Why relevant: Uses group symmetries for error mitigation; connects abelian/non-abelian structure to error correction efficiency.

10. **Error Dynamics in Affine Group Systems** (li_chen_2023_error_dynamics_affine.pdf)
    - Authors: Li, Chen, Zhang, Wei, Wu
    - Year: 2023
    - Why relevant: Error dynamics for affine group systems; extends log-linear framework to stochastic settings.
